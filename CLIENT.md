# Overview

Here you are to get a basic understanding of how to play on a custom Minecraft server.

### Client application

Download a client launcher: https://ru-m.org/

### Server configration

* Server: exnu.rial.cc
* Seed: 1049
* Difficulty: Hard
* View Distance: 10
* Session Timeout: 30 minutes
* Monsters * 2, animals * 2
